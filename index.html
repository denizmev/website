Research

Current Research

PhD Thesis Title: Real-time anxiety prediction in Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy

Abstract: The study sets out to integrate real-time anxiety prediction into Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy (VRET) for better decision-making. VRET is a therapy method that involves gradual exposure to a certain stimulus that the user is uncomfortable with, and couples this exposure with relaxation techniques. There have been many controlled experiments that provide evidence that VRET produces effective and long-lasting results. This study focuses on using wearable physiological sensors to collect information from the user and using a neural network to predict the anxiety level. This prediction can be used to enhance therapy by better tailoring it to the user’s needs.

Discord Chatbots

Two separate chatbots that are capable of processing text and responding in a meaningful way.

First one, "Llan" is programmed using discord.js library and GPT-3 API. It is good at answering general questions and creative tasks such as making ASCII art and writing poems. However, it does not remember conversations due to the price of GPT-3 API.

The second one, "Denizbot" is programmed using pycord library and GPT-J pretrained models. It is fine-tuned using LORA to speak similar to its creator, me. Although its capabilities of creativity and speech parsing is limited compared to Llan, it has more personality thanks to fine-tuning and can remember recent conversation.

node.js, discord.js, Pycord
