VR Experiment

Thank you for your interest in our study.

Our experiment investigates user state in Virtual Reality using wearable technology.

Given you choose to participate, you will be asked to complete two short questionnaires (~5 minute each) and a Virtual Reality colour-word association task (~15 minutes).

You can access the information sheet here for more information.

If you wish to participate or have further questions, please contact me directly via e-mail at d.mevlevioglu@cs.ucc.ie.

The experiment takes place in 1.30 in Western Gateway Building.
